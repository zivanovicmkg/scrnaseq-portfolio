# Project 05 — Colorectal Cancer Single-Cell Atlas

**Tool:** Scanpy
**Dataset (Option A):** Pelka et al. 2021 — CRC immune atlas, **GSE178341**
**Dataset (Option B):** Lee et al. 2020 — Korean CRC cohort, **GSE132465** + GSE144735
**References:**
- Pelka K, Hofree M, Chen JH, et al. *Spatially organized multicellular immune hubs in human colorectal cancer.* Cell 184, 4734–4752 (2021).
- Lee HO, Hong Y, Etlioglu HE, et al. *Lineage-dependent gene expression programs influence the immune landscape of colorectal cancer.* Nat Genet 52, 594–603 (2020).

## Goals
1. Build a multi-patient CRC atlas (epithelial + stromal + immune)
2. Identify MMR-deficient vs proficient signatures (if metadata available)
3. Demonstrate compositional analysis across patients

## Estimated effort
**15–20 hours.** Largest atlas of the set.
