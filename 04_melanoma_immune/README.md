# Project 04 — Melanoma Immune Landscape

**Tool:** Seurat (R)
**Dataset:** Jerby-Arnon et al. 2018 — melanoma + immune microenvironment
**Accession:** GEO **GSE115978**
**Reference:** Jerby-Arnon L, Shah P, Cuoco MS, et al. *A Cancer Cell Program Promotes T Cell Exclusion and Resistance to Checkpoint Blockade.* Cell 175, 984–997 (2018). DOI: 10.1016/j.cell.2018.09.006

## Why this project

Immuno-oncology niche — high client demand. Resistance-to-immunotherapy signature work is one of the most active scRNA-seq application areas in pharma.

## Goals
1. Map malignant vs. non-malignant compartments
2. Score the published "T cell exclusion" program
3. Characterize T cell exhaustion markers and infer ICB-resistance signature

## Estimated effort
**12–15 hours.**
